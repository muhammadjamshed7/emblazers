My Results Page (/curriculum/student/results)
Table:
| Quiz Title | Subject | Teacher | Date | Score | Grade | Status |
Grade badges with colors (A+ purple, A green, B blue, C yellow, D orange, F red)
Clicking any row → opens result detail modal showing per-question breakdown
Summary stats at top:

Average Grade
Best Score
Total Quizzes Attempted

My Fees Page (/curriculum/student/fees)
Pull data from existing feevouchers collection.
Table:
| Month | Total Amount | Paid | Balance | Due Date | Status |
Status badges: Paid (green) | Partial (yellow) | Pending (orange) | Overdue (red)
Summary at top:
Total Outstanding: Rs. 3,500  |  Total Paid This Year: Rs. 42,000
Note: This is READ-ONLY. No payment processing — just shows fee records.
Attendance Page (/curriculum/student/attendance)
Pull from existing attendancerecords collection.
Month-by-month table:
| Month | Present Days | Absent Days | Total Days | Attendance % |
Color code the percentage: ≥90% green, 75-89% yellow, <75% red
Overall attendance % shown as a circular progress indicator at top.
